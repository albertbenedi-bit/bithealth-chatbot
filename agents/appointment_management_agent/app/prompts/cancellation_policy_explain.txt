Our Appointment Cancellation Policy:

- We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling
- This allows us to offer the slot to other patients who need care
- Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) or no-shows may incur a fee
- Multiple no-shows may affect future booking privileges

To cancel or reschedule:
- Use our online portal
- Call our office during business hours
- Message through this chat system at least 24 hours in advance

We understand that emergencies happen. If you have an urgent situation, please contact us as soon as possible to discuss your options.
